welcome to a new open network of human knowledge

Seek and Share knowledge in a way that makes the world's wisdom more useful to you

How it works 

At the core of this Network is a very simple premise :
tell the world , in a short paragraph , something you wish to tell the world

this short note can be a thought that struck you ,
a good quote that you read ,
some advice on productivity ,

and after you type it down ,
select at least one other note on this network to link your note to ,
it is through these links that people will discover your note

How can you Learn here 

alternatively , come to this network to learn from those who have added notes to it ,
simply go to the Explore  page and search about any topic that interests you ,
things like productivity , philosophy are quite hot topics on the network ,

and over there you will see a list of notes that match your search term
select one that interests you the most , and go to its page

there you will find notes that are linked by the contributing users ,
No algorithms deciding which note you see next , what information you consume ,
Authors of this network link their notes to existing notes , and you see those links

Why it works

simply selecting one note to which to link ,
is a decision made by the user at the moment of authoring a note

when Author makes one or more connection to his note , it is a small local link ,
But over time , when more and more notes are added ,
and consequently more connections are made ,
A hundred notes later , what emerges is a network of interconnected notes
that are connected organically by the authors ,
and that contain paths that will delight you to read .

Go try some ,
Starting at any point in the network the user can discover knowledge ,
Lets say , you want to learn from the network community about
Productivity , or Buddhism or Neuroscience ,
Then simply use the search bar in the explore page and start down a reading path ,
choosing at each note , where to next .
Experience the Network

The Potential

Just like the way in a organism , individual cells , all performing simple tasks , 
taking part in simple interactions with their neighboring cells ,

theses individual cells and their networks when connected over a network become a organism 
that can perform more much more complex behaviors , then what a single cell could do .


On this network a small note with a local link  ,
when done a hundred times over by ten different people ,
will result in a network of knowledge greater than the 10 people .

This leverage of knowledge is what we are building here in this network
This Network does not have a fixed structure ,
The structure evolves over time ,
through the interactions of the people using it , and adding to it
This fundamentally different structure results in the emergence of a new type of connected knowledge ,

I urge you to imagine how this network could push further human wisdom in ways that will be difficult to even comprehend , 

These interconnected pathways in the network will eventually contain a lot more wisdom ,
than what one man could learn in one life time ,
This network will become a store of knowledge ,
Where you choose what to read next ,
And where each little note of knowledge also contains doors to more knowledge ,
