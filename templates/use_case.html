Use case Example

understanding the unique way in which this network can help you with structuring and communicating new ideas

Say , you wanted to tell everyone about a new project you are working on ,
for an example , the project is about how Sanskrit can be used as a programming language ,
because of its preciseness and completeness.


On the current internet you would have three options ,
    1. To find a place where the talk about new ways to create computer languages and tell them about your work
    2. To find a place where they talk about Sanskrit and its uses in the modern world  and tell them about your work
    3.  To tell about your work on all of these places individually and then track the conversation across all these different media


On this new knowledge sharing network you can share such knowledge ,
sharing knowledge , especially the types which cannot be put into only one particular category ,
or for knowledge for which there is no categorization invented yet ,

Here you simply need to create a note telling the world about what you
thought up , observed , invented , discovered ,
and link it to at least one other note in the network

In the above example , you could create a note about your Sanskrit based programming language ,
and link it to a note about how to make new programming language
and also link it to a note about the different things Sanskrit can be used for ,
To help your readers , you can even link further to a note where the details and specifics of the sanskrit grammar and syntax are described

With these 3 link your knowledge will be connected with all those discussions 
and whenever someone visits those notes , 
your note will be visible below as a connected note

When they want to add to the discussion , 
they simply create a note linked to your note ,
and they also link some other notes , to which they would like to draw your attention to .

Over time , when people discuss and add to the conversation , 
The simple act of linking at least one note that is connected to your note , 
On the larger perspective , results in a network of knowledge , 
connected by Users and not matching algorithms
