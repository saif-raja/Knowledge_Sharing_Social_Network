HI I am the creator of  Project Aphorisms , 

This project was originally intended to be a private note taking system , for organizing all the unstructured wisdom and insights , I came across from so many different sources , and also to store the few original thoughts I have , which I could not put into any boxes or categories 

A lot of this unstructured knowledge was getting  lost just because there was no easy way to organize and store it . I felt that this unstructured knowledge might be the one that was needed to be stored and remembered and revisited the most ,  just because it is so new , so on the edge of my mental categorization that , I didn't even have a box to store it in .

This burning desire , which consumed me over the 2-3 yrs before 2021 , led me to piece together a idea that would eventually become this network

I really hope this provide value to you and the world at large , I hope this becomes my contribution to the world
